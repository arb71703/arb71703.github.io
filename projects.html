Level Design

Geometry Dash features an expansive level editor comparable to high-end animation software. Over the past decade it has grown and enabled a large community to create a rich audio-visual storytelling medium.

The levels showcased here were designed entirely within the game’s level editor. This collection includes both independent projects and multi-creator collaborations, each planned and organized under my direction.
